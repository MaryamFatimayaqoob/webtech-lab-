About SweetBites

Founded in 2010, SweetBites Café & Bakery started as a small family-run bakery in Bakery Town. Over the past 16 years, we have grown into a beloved café known for quality, creativity, and a welcoming atmosphere.

Our Mission

Deliver high-quality baked goods, coffee, and a memorable experience to our customers every day.

Our Values

  • Quality - Only the freshest ingredients
  • Creativity - Seasonal flavors and artisanal designs
  • Community - Supporting local suppliers and farmers
  • Customer Happiness - Every customer leaves with a smile
